FAQ

Why do geeks under 18 need a consent form?
We’ll be documenting the event, so we need to ask your parent or guardian if you can be in pictures and videos— they can opt in or out on the form. We also want their permission to let you try our famous two-story slide! Two reasons: 1. So you can be famous 2. So you can go down our slide
How do I get the consent form?
Download it here and bring a signed copy with you. You can also grab one at the event (just make sure your parent or guardian is there to sign it).
What if I’m a student that’s 18 or older?
You don’t need a consent form. Just bring your ID.
I’m a parent, guardian, or teacher—what do I need to do?
If you’d like to take a tour (or go down our famous slide!) you’ll need to sign in at the front desk. Otherwise, you can head straight to the MGM conference room for the event.
Where exactly is the event?
The MGM conference room in the main building at MINDBODY. We’ll have signs and volunteers to help you find it.
Where can I park?
Anywhere in the MINDBODY lot or garage.
Will there be snacks or drinks?
Yep! There will also be plenty of swag.
