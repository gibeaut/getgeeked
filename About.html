About

Women in Tech (WIT) is a group of women and allies at MINDBODY who seek to educate others on equality, diversity, and inclusivity in the tech industry.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide women of all ages with the academic and professional resources to pursue lasting, fulfilling careers in tech.
